/// Why Nutrition Matters

  1. 1. Fuel Your Workouts

    Pre-Workout: Consume a balanced meal or snack with carbohydrates and protein to provide energy.

    Post-Workout: Replenish glycogen stores with carbohydrates and repair muscles with protein.

  2. 2. Hydration is Key

    Drink Water Regularly: Stay hydrated throughout the day, especially during workouts.

    Electrolyte Balance: Consider electrolyte-rich drinks, especially after intense workouts.

  3. 3. Protein Powerhouse

    Muscle Repair: Consume adequate protein to support muscle growth and repair.

    Lean Protein Sources: Include lean meats, poultry, fish, eggs, legumes, and dairy products.

  4. 4. Carbohydrate Choices

    Complex Carbs: Opt for complex carbohydrates like whole grains, fruits, and vegetables for sustained energy.

    Timing: Consume carbohydrates strategically, especially before and after workouts.

  5. 5. Healthy Fats

    Essential Fatty Acids: Incorporate healthy fats like avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil.

    Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Consider supplements if dietary intake is insufficient.
